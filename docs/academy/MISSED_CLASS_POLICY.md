# SwimBuddz Academy: Missed Class Policy

## 1. Safety First: The "Digital Catch-up"

If you miss a class for any reason (illness, work, travel), you must "catch up" mentally before getting back in the water.

- **Step 1:** Mark your absence in the SwimBuddz App.
- **Step 2:** Watch the **Video Lesson** for the week you missed. This will be automatically unlocked in your "My Progress" tab.
- **Step 3:** Arrive 1 hour early to your next class. Your coach will do a "dry-land" or shallow-water check to ensure you understand the key safety skills from the missed week.

## 2. Types of Absences

### ü§í The Early Cancel (Planned or Sudden)

- **Protocol:** Please mark yourself absent in the app as soon as you know. This frees up your spot for potential make-up students.
- **Make-up:** You receive **2 Floating Credits** per 12-week term. These credits can be used to book a spot in our special **"Saturday Open Practice"** sessions. These are coached sessions designed specifically to help you practice skills you missed or struggled with.

### üöë The Long-Term Absence (Injury/Emergency)

- **Protocol:** If you must miss **3 or more consecutive weeks** (e.g., due to injury), please contact support immediately.
- **Re-entry:** For your safety, you cannot jump back into a later week (e.g., Week 8) after missing a month of training. We will pause your membership and help you restart at Week 1 in a future cohort at no extra cost.

### ‚õàÔ∏è System Cancellations (Our Fault)

- **Protocol:** If _we_ cancel a class (e.g., pool maintenance, instructor illness), you are not penalized.
- **Resolution:** The term will either be extended by one week, or you will receive a pro-rated credit for your next renewal.

---

## 3. The "Two-Miss" Rule

If you miss **two consecutive classes** without a make-up session in between, we require a **Private Safety Check** (discounted rate) before you rejoin your group.

**Why?** Missing two weeks means missing significant safety milestones. This brief 1-on-1 session ensures you are safe to rejoin the group without anxiety or risk.
