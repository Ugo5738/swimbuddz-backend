## 3. COMPENSATION

### 3.1 Revenue Share Model

Coach shall be compensated on a **revenue share basis** as follows:
- Compensation is calculated as a percentage of collected learner fees for assigned cohorts
- The percentage is determined by Coach's grade level and program category as outlined in the SwimBuddz Coach Handbook
- Current revenue share percentage: **[PERCENTAGE]%** (Grade [X], [Category])

### 3.2 Payment Terms

- Payments are calculated per **4-week cohort block**
- Payment is based on **active enrolled learners** at the start of each block
- Payments are processed within **7 business days** after the end of each block
- Payments are made via **bank transfer from the SwimBuddz platform and payment processor account**
- Coach is responsible for providing accurate payment information on the platform

### 3.3 Payment Calculation

Payment for each block is calculated as:

```
Coach Payment = (Number of Active Learners × Learner Fee per Block) × Revenue Share Percentage
```

**Example:**
- 8 active learners × ₦50,000 per block = ₦400,000 total revenue
- Revenue share at 47% = ₦188,000 coach payment

### 3.4 Adjustments and Modifiers

Revenue share may be adjusted within the grade band based on:
- Cohort size exceeding standard limits
- Location or time slot difficulty
- New program pilot responsibilities
- Multi-cohort coordination
- Mentorship of junior coaches

All modifiers are documented in the Coach Operations Framework and applied at SwimBuddz's discretion.

### 3.5 Deductions

SwimBuddz may deduct from Coach payment:
- Equipment or materials provided and not returned
- Costs arising from Coach's breach of this Agreement
- Amounts owed to SwimBuddz under any other agreement

### 3.6 No Additional Compensation

Coach acknowledges that the revenue share constitutes full compensation for services rendered. No additional fees, bonuses, or reimbursements are payable except as explicitly agreed in writing.

## APPENDIX A: COACH CODE OF CONDUCT

### Our Commitment

SwimBuddz coaches are the face of our community. We trust you to uphold our values in every interaction.

### Core Principles

**1. Safety First**
- Never compromise on safety protocols
- Stop sessions if conditions are unsafe
- Report all incidents immediately
- Maintain current certifications

**2. Respect and Dignity**
- Treat all learners with respect regardless of ability, age, background, or progress
- Never shame, ridicule, or use aggressive coaching methods
- Be patient with fear, anxiety, and slow progress
- Celebrate effort, not just achievement

**3. Professionalism**
- Arrive on time and prepared
- Communicate schedule changes in advance
- Dress appropriately and maintain personal hygiene
- Keep personal matters separate from coaching

**4. Integrity**
- Be honest about learner progress
- Never collect payments directly without authorization
- Report conflicts of interest
- Honor confidentiality

**5. Community**
- Support fellow coaches
- Contribute to SwimBuddz culture
- Mentor junior coaches when asked
- Represent SwimBuddz positively

### Prohibited Conduct

The following are grounds for immediate termination:
- Any form of abuse (physical, verbal, emotional)
- Harassment or discrimination
- Coaching under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Falsifying records or documentation
- Unauthorized collection of payments
- Sharing confidential information
- Serious safety violations

- Anonymous reporting option available
